FAQ & Help

How do I sign up for multiple events?

If you plan to sign up for multiple events in one office visit, please take screenshots of those event's QR codes and bring them to the office for a fast registration

Can I sign up for my friends?

Yes! Please ask your friends to take screenshots of the QR-codes and bring them to the office, you can show them and sign your friends up that way.
If the event has online registration your friends will have to sign up on their app.

Where can I get more help?

If you have trouble using our system feel free to reach out to  webamster@esn-tumi.de.
We'll do our best to help you out.
