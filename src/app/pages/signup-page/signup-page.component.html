Get started

Why do I have to sign up?

In order to sign up for events we need to know who you are. So you can conveniently sign up here and enter your data so we can sign you up for events. Please note that an account is required for any event signup!
More info on this process can be found below the login options

By signing in I accept the Privacy Policy

It's recommended to use a social account instead of making a new one with your email

If you have forgotten your password, please contact us at webmaster@esn-tumi.de to get a reset.

How do I pay for an event?

If the event requires payment the app will tell you to sign up in our office. There we will collect the money and confirm your participation after you payed. You will then see the event in your upcoming events.

Do you share Data?

If you use a social account to log in, such as facebook or google we do not send any data to that service.
We strongly suggest to use a social login (blue buttons) in order to not create yet another account you have to remember.
