Get started

Why do I have to sign up?

In order to register for events you have to sign up here once and get a verified login. To get it just login and we will try to match your email with our database. Should the email match fail we'll request some additional info and then take a manual look at your case.

Once your account is active you can sign up for events and see the events you have coming up in the app.

How do I pay for an event?

If the event requires payment the app will tell you to sign up in our office. There we will collect the money and confirm your participation after you payed. You will then see the event in your upcoming events.

It's recommended to use a social account instead of making a new one with your email
