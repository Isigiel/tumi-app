TUMi?

We take care of international students

Every semester we organize the orientation weeks for incoming exchange students to get them started at TUM and in Munich.
We also hand out the student IDs and help out with problems coming up.

We organize events throughout the semester

Every semester we organize more than 200 Events, from 5-day road trips to Alpaca hikes. Every TUM student will be able to find something interesting to them and is invited to become one of our 19k participants.

We want you on board

All the things we do are only possible because of the 200+ voluntary TUMi tutors. Every semester we are looking for new students to come and join the TUMi family, take part in awesome events and meet people from all over the world.

Applications open in January

We're not in this alone

We are part of TUM

We are a direct part of the TUM international office, This allows us to offer you even more diverse and affordable events with a lot of support by the university. It enables us to have very consistent office hours and secure rooms for various activities

We are part of ESN

TUMi is an official ESN-section. ESN is the Erasmus Student Network, an European organization taking care of all things Erasmus and supporting local section on their quest to provide an awesome experience exchange students
