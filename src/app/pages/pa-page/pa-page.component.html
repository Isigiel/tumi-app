Party Animals

You want to start the exchange on a high note, get to know other students from all around the world and have your first two weeks all planned for you? Then we have something for you: The Party Animals Package!

The Gang

You’ll be assigned to a group of 20 exchange students and 5 tutors. With this group of 25 amazing people, you’ll spend your first two weeks exploring Munich. The program might be for two weeks, but you’ll remain friends for life.

Get to know bavarian culture

Let's not kid ourselves, the best way to make new friends is to have one, or two, or more beers together. At party animals we wil make sure that you're very familiar with bavarian beer culture after two weeks.

Things are looking greater

This time will be better than ever before, marvel at your newly designed shirts and be part of never before seen party groups.
To kick everything off we will spend a night at Wernfels Castle. Yes, you read it right a 13th century castle next to Nuremberg. While on other days in Munich we have other things coming up: a brewery tour, the Beerlympics and epic parties!
Rest assured, you’ll never get bored.
